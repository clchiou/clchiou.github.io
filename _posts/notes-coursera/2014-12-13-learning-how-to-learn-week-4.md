Test Preparation
----------------

* Team work, study group.
* **Practice.**
* **Teach.**
* Start from hard questions, but quickly jump to easy ones.
  - Use focused---diffuse modes, background processing.
* Use metaphor and analogy.
* Don't multitasking.
* **Physical exercise** makes you a better learner.
