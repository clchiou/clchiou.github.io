h3. Summary

* $0, 1, +$ and set theory...
* $\Rightarrow \mathbb{N},$
* $\Rightarrow \mathbb{Z},$
* $\Rightarrow \mathbb{Q}$ (lack completeness),
* $\Rightarrow$ Construction of $\mathbb{R},$
* $\Rightarrow$ Completeness of $\mathbb{R}$ $=$ Bolzano-Weierstrass Theorem $=$ Nested Interval Theorem.

h4. Branch: Fundamental Theorem of Calculus

* Completeness of $\mathbb{R}...$
* $\Rightarrow$ Properties of continuous functions, "Mid-Value" Theorem, Extreme Value Theorem,
* $\Rightarrow$ Rolle's Theorem, Mean Value Theorem, Mean Value Theorem for Integration,
* $\Rightarrow$ Fundamental Theorem of Calculus.

h4. Branch: Existence of Riemann integral

* Completeness of $\mathbb{R}...$
* $\Rightarrow$ Heine-Borel Theorem,
* $\Rightarrow$ Uniformly Continuous Theorem,
* $\Rightarrow$ Heine-Borel Theorem,
* $\Rightarrow$ Existence of Riemann integral.
